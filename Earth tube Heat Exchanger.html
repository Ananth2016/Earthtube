Firstly, we need to manufacture a rectangular wooden box and fill it withsand. Next, we insert a condenser-type coil into the box. We then attach electrical pipes to the corners of the wooden box and secure them with nails.A blower is attached to one end of the condenser-type coil, while the other end is connected to a room or a form house, etc. We pour water through the electrical pipes and, after thermal resistance is established between the sand and water, cool air is delivered through one end of the coil during the summer season. The main purpose of This project is to provide cool air during the summer and warm air during the winter. This system is also known as an "Underground Water Tube Heat Exchanger1."
